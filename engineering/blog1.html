First Blog: New Engineering Portfolio

2/13/2019

Today is February 13, 2019, and I have finished re-modeling my Engineering Portfolio. It used to look like a pretty cramped up mess where my entire home-page was filled up with way too many pictures and words/sentences which were very scattered and disorganized. The feedback I got for that website has was pretty positive as many people liked my fancy animations that moved words around. Although my friend Anshul(GitHub: Anshul2004) told me that my website was pretty messy and that I needed to fix it and so here I have it, a completely remodeled and fixed Engineering Portfolio filled with animations, HD pictures, and most importantly good spacing between the elements of the site and not that much white space left over.

Now let me walk through the additions of my website. First I added an absolute fixed background image to each of my web pages from a website called www.unsplash.com. Next, I added a typewriter-based animation text for my home screen to introduce the website's name. Finally, I added various Javascript and JQuery scripts to my website to make it more appealing to visitors and future job employers. At the end I fixed my footer to make it neater, more spaced out and I added a copyright logo. All together I spent over 10 hours in 2 days re-making my entire webpage. All that time has paid off because I am satisfied with my work but I am still hungry to add more to this webpage. The grind will never stop ;). I will post on my personal blog at least once a week for probably forever so please enjoy and feel free to look around the rest of my website
