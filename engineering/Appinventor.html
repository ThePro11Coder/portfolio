MIT Appinventor app: State Geography Quiz

9/18/2018 - 10/11/2018

The Objective of this project was to create an interactive capitals and states quiz for kids to study for their Geography quizes. During this project My partner and I didn't split roles. We worked on the Same thing together until the task we wanted to do was finished. We first finished the project all together then we finished documentation before testing out and making some additional changes needed for the app. Our communication system was very effiecient because we both used email to contact each other and we both frequently check our emails. Challenges we faced were in the coding like when we had to figure out how to make our app lag less while playing. But overall the design process was very smooth.
