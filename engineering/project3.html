Image Manipulation Project: Pictopia

3/1/2019 - 3/24/2019

The objective of this project was to create a program that could turn a family or memorable picture into painting-esque like. During this project Anshul and I did pair-programming. We split the program into many pieces where I would work on one fucntion and would work on the other function. We finished the programming then went to do the documentation. With many userful resources at our disposal we were able to make our documentation look as good as our program proffesionally. Our communication during this project was very strong. We exchanged phone numbers and emails and so through that we were able to work at home frequently. Challenges we had were mainly in the programming. There were many bugs we had to fix such as the color-sheme and formt changer. Other than those bugs our design process was pretty smooth.
